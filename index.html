How to Use

Thank you so much for using our app! Here is a quick guide on how to get started: Step 1: On the home page, you will see a text box at the very top. Type or paste the Malayalam text you want to convert into that box. Step 2: Click the "Translate" button located at the bottom of the page. Step 3: Your converted text will instantly appear in the "Manglish" result box below. Step 4: You can easily copy the result from there and paste it wherever you want.
